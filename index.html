BIO

Cinzia Palumbo is a graphic design student attending St.Clair College from Belle River Ontario. She has a background in construction and beer because of her previous work as a lumber desk associate at the local lumber yard, and work at "The Beer Store." She also has interests in political science as that was her area of study at the University of Windsor. Cinzia has been a traditional artist for many years and wanted to move into using these skills as a full time career. She has special skills in bold illustrations especially character design, social posts, mockups, logos, and brand creation, product design, motion and video editing.

Her favourite thing is creating a new brand from start to finish including logos, colour palette, business cards, staff badges, shirts and more! Cinzia also enjoys a good drink with friends, social events to meet new people, video games and music, that she uses for new ides in her work and personal ventures. Cinzia is very outgoing, kind, and loves to work with new people to widen her experiences in different fields and leanrning new skills.
